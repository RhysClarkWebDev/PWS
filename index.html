METHOD

Initial Consultation

When you get in touch with us our first step is an initial consultation to get to know you, the property and the aspiration for your project. We believe every scheme should be tailored to its occupants, its site, intended usage and design aspirations. We will always endeavour to meet with you in person on site for this reason. This is also a great opportunity for you to discuss any ideas and for you to get to know your designer.

Survey and Design

We, or an appointed surveyor, will undertake a detailed survey of your house or site so that we can accurately and reliably design around your existing property and needs. We use a mix of 2D and 3D to design all projects from sketch to photorealistic and even VR to help you visualise and understand the designs.

Planning through to Completion

We provide fees by stage to allow you to decide how far you take us on your journey. We start the process with thoroughly thought out concepts. These are carried forward whilst we develop them down to the smallest details if required. We can coordinate with; planners, building control, engineers, consultants and builders on your behalf. We are keen to be with you and your scheme throughout the process.
